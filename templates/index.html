About US

The TAFS MUN Society was started with an aim to initiate learning which does not take place within the walls of a normal classroom.

Like the preceding years, this year as well we start this journey with an aim to recognize the significance of the United Nations Organization and the exemplary role played by it in fostering deliberation and policy formation, but at the same time, we also aim to realize the imperative role played by the Non-UN organizations, forums and of certain events which have shaped history and define our existence today.
